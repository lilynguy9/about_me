Research

Currently, I work as a Research Technician at the Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology Graduate University (OIST). In the Marine BioPhysics Unit led by Dr. Satoshi Mitarai, I analyze bubble dynamics data collected from surface autonomous vehicles deployed during typhoon season around the Okinawa islands. I also support upcoming research on wave-driven rip currents to better understand water safety risks, in cooporation with the Japan Coast Guard. In addition to data analysis, I assist with fieldwork and provide technical support for various research activities within the unit.

Research Interests: Ocean wave mechanics, fluid dynamics, air-sea interations, remote sensing, bubble plumes, coastal engineering, rip currents, oceanography
